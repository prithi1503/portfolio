Project Description

An e-portfolio is a digital collection of a student’s work, achievements, and learning experiences. It helps in documenting progress made during a course or program. The purpose of this e-portfolio is to showcase my understanding of ICT and its application in education. It provides evidence of academic work such as assignments, presentations, and projects completed during the course. E-portfolios encourage reflection, self-assessment, and continuous improvement, and help future teachers demonstrate professional competencies.
